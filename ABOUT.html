About This Platform

Welcome to the University of Zambia (UNZA) 2024 Thesis and Dissertation Compliance Checker.

This platform is designed to assist postgraduate students in ensuring that their manuscripts align with the UNZA 2024 postgraduate guidelines. The system automatically highlights sections of your manuscript that do not comply with the specified requirements, making it easier for you to correct these issues before submission.

Our goal is to streamline the submission process, helping students meet the necessary standards efficiently and accurately. The platform checks for various compliance factors including format, structure, page numbering, and content limits, ensuring that your work meets the highest academic standards.
